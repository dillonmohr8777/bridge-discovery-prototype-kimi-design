# Bridge product and marketing context

Status: working discovery context; client approval required.

## Product

Bridge is a browser-based cannabis industry directory and professional network. The Phase 1 concept connects brands, dispensaries, retailers, sales representatives, and administrators through searchable profiles, business/license verification, saved profiles, basic announcements, notifications, and structured contact requests.

## Positioning hypothesis

For cannabis professionals who need credible B2B relationships, Bridge is a verified industry network that turns fragmented discovery into clear, intentional introductions. Unlike broad social networks or unverified directories, Bridge is structured around business roles, markets, verification, and permission-based contact.

This is a hypothesis for Tori to confirm, not approved public copy.

## Audience jobs

- Brands: become discoverable and find retail, distribution, or representation partners.
- Dispensaries/retailers: assess credible brands and representatives and request connections.
- Sales representatives: show territory and experience and find operators needing representation.
- Administrators: verify evidence, approve profiles, and moderate activity.
